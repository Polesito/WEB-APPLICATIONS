My super poem

In the realm of cyberspace, where pixels dance and glow,
There lies a language, both mighty and low.
HTML, the architect of web's grand design,
With tags and attributes, it crafts a digital shrine.

Beneath its surface, a world unfurls,
Where creators and dreamers, unfettered, twirls.
With each angle bracket and closing slash,
HTML weaves stories, in a digital flash.

<Header> it declares, with a flourish and flair,
Announcing the beginning, with elegance rare.
<Body> it breathes life, into the blank screen,
A canvas for visions, both vivid and serene.

With <p> for paragraphs, and <div> for division,
HTML orchestrates, with precision and vision.
Lists, links, and images, it beckons to arrange,
In a tapestry of knowledge, ever ready to exchange.

From <table> to structure, data it organizes,
Charts and graphs, it elegantly devises.
Forms it molds, for interaction profound,
HTML stands firm, on digital ground.

Yet beyond its function, lies a deeper grace,
HTML connects hearts, across time and space.
Uniting minds, in a symphony of code,
It bridges divides, with each webpage it strode.

So let us honor, this language profound,
For in its simplicity, virtues abound.
HTML, the foundation, on which we build,
A testament to creativity, forever thrilled.
